FAMH4010A - Managing Health Projects

Journey Through Project Management

This portfolio documents my growth and learning throughout the Managing Health Projects course. From early understandings to comprehensive reflection, I've developed skills and insights that have transformed my approach to healthcare leadership.

Key Learning Areas

  • Responsible Leadership: Evolved from seeing leadership as merely decision-making to understanding it as relational, involving trust, vulnerability, and humility.
  • Systems Thinking: Developed appreciation for how health systems interconnect beyond clinical settings to include social determinants, policy, and community relationships.
  • Project Methodologies: Learned to select and adapt approaches based on specific project needs rather than applying one-size-fits-all solutions.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Discovered the importance of early and continuous stakeholder involvement throughout the project lifecycle.

Module Reflections Summary

Through seven comprehensive modules, I gained practical insights into every aspect of project management:

  • Gained confidence in mixing methodologies to suit different project needs
  • Learned the importance of thorough initiation and stakeholder analysis
  • Recognized procurement as central to project success rather than just a technical detail
  • Understood documentation as a vital communication tool, not merely administrative work
  • Appreciated project closure as an opportunity for learning and celebration

These modules have helped me develop a more structured approach while maintaining flexibility and responsiveness to changing healthcare environments.

Personal Development

My journey involved significant personal growth in key leadership competencies:

  • Communication: Developed strategies for clear messaging and active listening
  • Adaptive Problem-Solving: Built capacity to navigate uncertainty and complexity
  • Emotional Intelligence: Improved awareness of how I respond to stress and conflict
  • Systems Thinking: Enhanced ability to identify root causes and interconnections

Through resources like "Crucial Conversations" and training in Agile leadership, I've shifted from focusing on "fixing" perceived weaknesses to cultivating an adaptive, growth-oriented mindset.
