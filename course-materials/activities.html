Learning Activities

Below you'll find the key activities completed during the Contemporary Health module. Each activity represents a step in developing a comprehensive understanding of health systems and their complexities.

Choosing a Local Health Focus

Critical Analysis Local Context

Exploring a specific health challenge in South Africa and understanding how it connects to broader social, economic, and historical contexts.

View Activity

Context Analysis

Systems Thinking Contextual Awareness

Analyzing the systemic context of health challenges and understanding the interplay between various factors that influence health outcomes.

View Activity

Exploring Policy

Policy Analysis Governance

Investigating the policy landscape surrounding health issues, identifying gaps, and understanding how policies shape healthcare delivery and access.

View Activity

Mapping with Appreciation

Appreciative Inquiry Asset-Based Approach

Using Appreciative Inquiry to approach health challenges, focusing on strengths and opportunities rather than solely on problems.

View Activity

Understanding HSS

Conceptual Understanding Theory

Developing a personal understanding of Health Systems Science as a field of study and practice, exploring its core principles and applications.

View Activity

Racism & the Right to Health

Health Equity Social Justice

Exploring the intersection of racism and health rights, examining how systemic inequities impact access to care and health outcomes.
