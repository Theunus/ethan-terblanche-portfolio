Forward Plan — Learning Goals (Next 3–6 Months)

Learning Goals (Next 3–6 Months)

  1. Health Data Governance & POPIA Compliance
    Deepen my understanding of privacy-by-design principles and develop practical skills to conduct a data protection impact assessment, ensuring that future pilots meet regulatory and ethical standards.
  2. Applied Analytics & Evaluation
    Build capacity to design and execute mixed-methods evaluations, including KPI dashboards and quasi-experimental assessments, to measure the impact of MediMatch on placement satisfaction and rural retention.
  3. Stakeholder Negotiation & Engagement
    Strengthen my ability to facilitate consensus with universities, councils, and the Department of Health by practising structured negotiation techniques and developing standardised partnership packs.
  4. Venture Finance Literacy
    Advance my financial acumen by refining cost models, conducting pricing sensitivity analyses, and preparing short- and medium-term cash runway scenarios to support scaling decisions.
  5. Strategic Communication & Influence
    Enhance my communication craftsmanship through the iterative redesign of the MediMatch pitch, development of policy briefs, and creation of targeted messages for diverse stakeholder groups.

Planned Artefacts to Create/Add

  • Pilot Protocol: A structured document defining KPIs, ethical approvals, data handling procedures, and evaluation methodology.
  • Stakeholder Interview Digest: A thematic summary of insights from universities, interns, and policymakers, including direct quotations and adoption signals.
  • Revised Financial Model: A bottom-up cost and revenue framework with sensitivity testing and multiple pricing bands.
  • Adoption Case Study: A narrative and evidence-based account of the first pilot cohort’s outcomes, highlighting testimonials and measurable improvements.

Closing Statement

This portfolio illustrates a transformative learning journey — from crafting a persuasive problem–solution narrative (Pitch), to mapping systemic complexity (Ecosystem), to producing an integrated and actionable venture design (Proposal). Together, these artefacts evidence my growth as a systems thinker, reflective practitioner, and entrepreneurial change agent. Looking ahead, I am equipped not only to refine MediMatch but also to contribute meaningfully to innovation in healthcare workforce planning.
